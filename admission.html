Grade-XI Entrance Examination and Admission Information (2079/80)
Admission to CCRC is selective based on your past accomplishments and
future potential. Your academic records of SEE level, standardized 
 CCRC’s entrance exam results, and counselor’s/ teacher’s recommendation 
 are the bases for the determination of your admission at CCRC.


Eligibility Criteria :

Science : Minimum 2.8 GPA, 
          C+ in Science , Mathematics & English;
          D+ in other subjects (Optional Mathematics is compulsory) 

Management/Humanities/Law : Minimum 2.0 GPA, 
                            D+ in English, Mathematics, Social Studies & Nepali.

Only regular SEE students of this year can apply.
